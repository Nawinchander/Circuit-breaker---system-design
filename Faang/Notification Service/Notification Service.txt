Circuit Breaker for Notification Service
Scenario:

User signup triggers:

SMS service
Email service
Push service

If SMS provider fails:

do not block signup
fallback to email

Used in:

Meta notifications
LinkedIn alerts
Swiggy OTP delivery

Signup → SMS Service
      ↘ fail → Email Service


Interview Point

Critical because:

Notification failure must not fail signup

This shows graceful degradation.
